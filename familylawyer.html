    • FAMILY

    Advocate Ajay Vadhiya understands that family law issues can be highly emotional and complex. He offers compassionate and dedicated legal representation to clients in Ahmedabad facing various family law matters.

    Here's how Advocate Vadhiya can assist you:

    • Divorce
      • Contested and Uncontested Divorce: Advocate Vadhiya will guide you through the legal process, whether you and your spouse agree on the terms or need assistance navigating a more complex situation.
      • Property Division:He will ensure fair and equitable division of marital assets and debts, considering various factors like prenuptial agreements (if applicable).
      • Spousal Support:Advocate AJAY will fight for fair spousal support arrangements based on each party's financial circumstances.
      • Child Custody and Visitation: He will advocate for your parental rights and strive for a child custody and visitation agreement that prioritises the best interests of your children.
    • Child Support:Advocate Vadhiya will help you establish or modify child support orders ensuring your children receive the financial resources they need.
    • Prenuptial Agreements:Advocate Vadhiya can guide you in drafting a prenuptial agreement to protect your assets and interests before marriage.
    • Domestic Violence:Advocate Vadhiya will represent you in obtaining restraining orders and pursuing legal action against an abusive spouse.
    • Paternity:He can assist in establishing paternity or contesting a paternity claim to ensure child support and visitation rights are determined accurately.

    Why Choose Advocate Ajay Vadhiya for Your Family Law Needs?

    • Compassionate and Understanding:Advocate Vadhiya recognizes the emotional toll of family law matters. He approaches each case with empathy and sensitivity.
    • Skilled Negotiator:He strives to resolve disputes outside of court whenever possible, saving you time and money. However, he is also a strong advocate in court when necessary.
    • Focus on the Best Interests of Children: In child custody and support cases, Advocate Vadhiya prioritises the well-being and best interests of your children.
    • Clear Communication: He keeps you informed throughout the legal process, explaining options and next steps in a clear and concise manner.
